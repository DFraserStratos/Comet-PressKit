Comet - Press Kit

A captivating game for the Playdate handheld gaming system.

About the Game

Comet is an engaging game developed for the Playdate gaming system. Created with passion by indie developers, the game has received strong support from the Playdate Squad discord community throughout its development process.

Development

The game has been developed with careful attention to the unique features of the Playdate, including its distinctive black and white display and crank mechanism. The development journey has been documented on the Playdate Developer Forum, showing the evolution of the game from concept to reality.
